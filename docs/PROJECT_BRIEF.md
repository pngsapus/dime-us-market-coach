# Project Brief

Dime US Market Coach is a beginner-friendly US stock analysis assistant for users who manually buy and sell US stocks through the Dime app.

The app helps users understand market context, follow stocks, review risk, create simulated practice plans, check whether a real Dime price still fits the plan, and record lessons in a journal.

This is not an auto-trading app. It must not place trading orders or use direct trading-signal language.
